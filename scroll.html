SPECTRE

THE NEXT GENERATION OF ROLLS-ROYCE

The most powerful Rolls-Royce ever — a spiritual successor to the Phantom Coupé and an emblem of artisanship in perfect harmony with electric technology.

BESPOKE INSTRUMENT DIALS

Seen for the first time on any Rolls-Royce, fully digital Bespoke Instrument Dials are a stunning display of technological craftsmanship, indicative of Spectre’s digital identity and the marque’s unparalleled design proficiency. Spectre owners may select a personalised and complementary dial colour upon commission. Modernising the view from the driver’s seat, the dials’ hands are decorated with a trail of brilliant stars — an enchanting celestial connection to the Starlight Doors, Starlight Headliner, and Illuminated Fascia.

A CELESTIAL ENTRANCE

A spellbinding option for the interior, Starlight Doors radiate with 4,796 backlit perforations. Each star is positioned by hand, creating a subtle sense of motion within Spectre’s cabin. The constellations form a luminous symphony with the Starlight Headliner and Illuminated Fascia, resulting in an immersive, 360 experience that transcends the boundaries of this celestial dimension.

ILLUMINATED FASCIA

Evoking feelings of wonder within the interior, the Illuminated Fascia mesmerises with over 5,500 stars depicting the Spirit of Ecstasy’s ‘wing’. The effect accentuates the radiant wordmark within. Vibrant against a Piano Black background, the design adjusts to the interior lighting and enlivens when the doors are opened from the outside. There are many distinguishing marks that set a Rolls-Royce apart, and the fascia — a space reserved for inspired expression — is embellished with inventive elements integral to the marque. A CANOPY OF STARS Rolls-Royce’s Starlight Headliner has been a statement feature since its 2007 debut, transfixing passengers with the atmospheric glow and hues of delicately placed fibre-optic lights. Ablaze with an intricate show of shooting stars, this stellar sky continues to shine for Spectre, remaining an iconic and timeless Rolls-Royce feature. EXQUISITE WOODWORK A Rolls-Royce craftsperson spends large stretches of time perfecting the smallest of details, informed by years of ardent training. Woodwork is no exception, with decorative pieces requiring an unwavering level of precision and skill. Interior open-pore veneer is testament to this. The wood’s bold grain folds texture into the interior — balancing the clean, refined, and digitised surfaces found with the cabin.

ICONIC HEADLIGHTS

Rolls-Rocye's iconic split headights have not been seen since Spectre's spiritual predecessor, the Phantom Coupe. Spectre builds upon the original, xisually arresting design with two components - upper daytime running lights and a lower, fourlamp cluster that brightly illuminates the road ahead.

SPIRIT OF ECSTASY

The Spirit of Ecstasy embodies the marques audacious and intrepid sense of evolution. The new design depicts her riding the wind at a lower stance, her reimagined robes flow delicately in her wake. These subtle enhancements modernise our emblem; an aerodynamic muse - for who speed is an intrinsic extension of her grace.

The World's first ultra-luxury electric super coupe is an emblem of artisanship in harmony with electric technology.
